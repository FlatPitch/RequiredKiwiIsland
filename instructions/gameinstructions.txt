Game Objectives:
	- Count all the Kiwis
	- Trap all Predators
Use the buttons to move around the map.
Use the object viewer to interact with certain objects
Keep on eye on your stamina, food items will replenish your energy
Watch out for hazards, they will reduce your stamina but some can be fatal!
Broken traps can be repaired with the screwdriver
Win the game to get a voucher!

Key:
K - Kiwi
P - Predator
H - Hazard
T - Tool
E - Food
F - Fauna
