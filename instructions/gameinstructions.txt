Game Objectives:
	- Count all the Kiwis
	- Trap all Predators
Use the A_D (left-right), W_S (up-down) buttons to move around the map.
Use the object viewer to interact with certain objects
Use the inventory viewer to use traps or food
Keep on eye on your stamina, food items will replenish your energy
There are hazards that are hidden, be careful as they will reduce your stamina!
Broken traps can be repaired with the screwdriver
Win the game to get a voucher!

Select a Kiwi or Predator in the Objects field to see description,
Click on the Image to get a chance to answer a question for extra,
stamina.
